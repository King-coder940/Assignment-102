BMI Calculator

A BMI calculator estimates body fat based on weight and height. It helps determine if someone is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Calculation Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 - and Above Obese

Calculate your BMI

To calculate BMI, divide your weight in kilograms by your height in meters squared. The formula is: BMI = weight (kg) / height² (m²).
